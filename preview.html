Pasta al pomodoro

A quick and light dish, garnished with fresh basil, parsley and a drizzle of pesto.

Ingredients

  • 400 grams
    Dried Linguine
  • 250 grams
    Cherry Tomatoes
    Halved
  • 2 Onions
    Finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of Garlic (crushed)
  • 500 grams
    Passata
    recipe in book
  • 100 grams
    Green Pesto
    recipe in book
  • 100 grams
    grated Italian hard cheese
  • 200 grams
    Creme Fraiche
  • 1 tablespoon
    Olive Oil
  • 4 tablespoons
    Basil leaves (roughly chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons
    Balsamic Vinegar
  • To taste
    Sea Salt
    Black Pepper

Instructions

Make the Pasta

Get your pot boiling with salted water and add the linguine when it's bubbling with excitement! After 12 minutes, give it a break and drain the water. Add a little olive oil to keep things smooth and prevent sticking.

Prepare the vegetables

First, halve the cherry tomatoes and place them in a bowl. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and sugar over them - they'll love it! Finely dice the onion and add a splash of water to keep it fresh and keep the tears away. Chop the garlic finely, mix it with the onion, and set aside. Finally, roughly chop the basil leaves.

Make the Pasta Sauce

Get your frying pan sizzling with a splash of olive oil. Toss in the garlic and onion, letting them dance and soften for a few minutes. Now, add those lovely halved cherry tomatoes and fragrant basil, and let them mingle and cook for about 6-8 minutes. Time to bring in the creamy goodness - pour in the creme fraiche, balsamic vinegar, and passata, and let it all simmer together for a cozy 5 minutes. Give it a taste, sprinkle in some salt and pepper if needed, and then lovingly coat your pasta with this delightful sauce using tongs.

Serve and Enjoy!

Time to plate up! Spread the pasta onto plates and add a dollop of vibrant green pesto. Sprinkle generously with grated cheese. E ora si mangia! Enjoy your culinary creation!
Pro tip: Serve with a crispy slice of toasted bread or a crusty baguette for an extra treat!
